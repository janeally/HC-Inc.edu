Registration and Enrollment

The Registration and enrollment of students are covered by the following policies:


1. A student is considered officially enrolled only after he/she has has complied the following

a.) Submitted all required admission credentials and completed all specific program requirements

b.) Paid in cash or made and initial payment of school fees

c.) Been authorized to attend classes (usually evidenced by securing his/her registration form, submits class cards to the teacher, and his/her name appears in the official class list for the term)

3.2 When a student is officially enrolled, it is understood that he/she is enrolled for the duration of the entire term or semester, regardless of whether or not school fees have been fully paid

3.3 Asidery from the specified registration period, a student may be allowed to enroll in accordance with rules of the college for late enrollment but shall in no case exceed two weeks after the opening of classes

SCHOOL FEES

Terms of payment can be made on:


1.1 Cash Basis, which offers a discount of ten percent (10%) when full payment is made upon enrolment

1.2 Installment Basis, periodic/quarterly payment of Tuition & Miscellaneous Fees.

1.3 Credit Card: Full payment through credit student shall be given a 5% discount. Installment payment the same policy applies.

1.4 Assestment notices will be issued in advance for payers to meet the scheduled examination permit.

1.5 Promissiory Note. Student who failed to settle their Account due/s on the specific period may be allowed to apply for a "promissory Permit" subject to the approval of the Vice President for Administration or Chief Finance Office.

1.6 Arrears. Whenever a student has unsettled accountor arrears fromthe previous term/academic year, he/she cannot enroll for the following term/academic year unless these accounts or arrears have been settled.
